How to Use .CallPhone Domain Links

Callphone links are powered by the handshake blockchain which isn't yet supported by traditional DNS. To use your .Callphone domain simply add .hns.to to the end. HNS.to is a bridge that makes any handshake domain useable without installing handshake domain viewers.

Examples: johnsmith.callphone.hns.to, johnsplumbers.callphone.hns.to
